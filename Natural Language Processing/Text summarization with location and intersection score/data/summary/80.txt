Vikings -- known for their fearsome conquests -- first came to North America a thousand years ago, according to historians.

Now scientists may be able to shed more light on that history with satellite images that reveal what may be a Norse site in North America.

The potential site could provide information on how long Vikings stayed in North America and provide other information about the group.

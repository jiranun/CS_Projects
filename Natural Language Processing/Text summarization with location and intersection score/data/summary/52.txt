"I'm just thinking of that day when we left (Cuba) and shaking like a leaf," she said.

Ana Garcia was crying and could hardly walk as she left the cruise ship that took her from Miami to Havana.

Garcia is one of about 700 passengers on the first cruise in decades to travel from the United States to Cuba.

The Adonia, a ship on Carnival Corp.'s Fathom cruise line, docked Monday in Havana.

The 52-year-old was 4 when she left Cuba with her sister and parents.
